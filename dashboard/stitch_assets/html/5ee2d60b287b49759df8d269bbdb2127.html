Image Classification with Convolutional Neural Networks

Problem Statement

Construct a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) capable of classifying handwritten digits. The network must extract hierarchical spatial features using convolutional layers before flattening the representation for a final dense classification layer.

Your objective is to design an architecture $f(x)$ that minimizes the categorical cross-entropy loss $\mathcal{L}$ over the training set, achieving a validation accuracy of at least 98%.

$$ \mathcal{L} = -\sum_{c=1}^{M} y_{o,c} \log(p_{o,c}) $$
